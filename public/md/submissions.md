# Instructions

## How to Login
Please enter the phone number of any one participant who is a registered part of your team to log in. This is only for authentication purposes and does not make any difference.

## General Rules for All Categories

1. **Submission Deadline**  
   - All projects must be submitted by the specified deadline. Late submissions will not be accepted.

2. **Conduct During Submission**  
   - Submissions must be original work; plagiarism will lead to disqualification.

3. **Results and Feedback**  
   - Results will be announced on the official competition website, and feedback will be provided based on the evaluation criteria.

4. **Disputes and Appeals**  
   - Any disputes regarding submissions must be submitted in writing within 24 hours of results announcement.

## Kanvas Rules
**Eligibility**  
- Participants must submit a digital or physical artwork based on the provided topics.  
- Each team can submit only one project.

**Submission Format**  
- Digital submissions must be in PNG or JPEG format.  
- Physical posters must be scanned and submitted as a high-resolution image.  

**Documentation**  
- A brief explanation of the artwork, including its inspiration and relevance to the topic.

**Topics**  
- Meme-Evolution: Create a meme capturing the essence of Indian politics today.  
- An Ode to Shadow: Create an artwork emphasizing the beauty of shadow over light.  
- Reinterpreting the Masters: A modern twist on a famous work from the Renaissance, Baroque, or Impressionist periods.

**Review Criteria**  
- Creativity and originality  
- Relevance to the chosen topic  
- Artistic technique and overall presentation

## Cinematique Rules

- Each team can submit only one project.

**Submission Format**

**Topics**

- Haunted Past: Dare to venture into the unknown.  
- Seedlings to Saplings: Small beginnings, strong foundations.

**Review Criteria**  
- Storytelling and creativity  
- Cinematography and editing quality  
- Execution of the chosen theme

## Pitchathon Rules
**Eligibility**  
- Participants must present a viable startup idea.  
- Each team can submit only one project.

**Submission Format**  
- A presentation file (PPTX or PDF) summarizing the startup idea.  
- Supporting materials, including a business plan and prototype (if applicable).  

**Documentation**  
- A business plan outlining:  
  - Executive summary  
  - Market analysis  
  - Revenue model  
  - Go-to-market strategy  

**Tracks**  
- Agri-Tech, Fin-Tech, Ed-Tech, Cybersecurity, AI & ML, Green-Tech, Logistics.

**Review Criteria**  
- Clarity and feasibility of the business idea  
- Market potential and competitive analysis  
- Quality and creativity of the pitch

## Web Waves Rules
**Eligibility**  
- Participants must design a website on one of the given topics.  
- Each team can submit only one project.

**Submission Format**  
- Submissions must include the URL of the deployed website.

**Review Criteria**
